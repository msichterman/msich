## Welcome

This is the beginning of something new. I've been meaning to start writing for a while now, and I'm excited to finally have a space to share my thoughts.

I'll be writing about software engineering, entrepreneurship, and the things I'm learning along the way. Expect posts about building products, leading engineering teams, and the occasional deep dive into a technology that has caught my attention.

- **Engineering**: Technical deep dives, architecture decisions, and lessons learned from building products at scale.
- **Entrepreneurship**: Stories and reflections from building [Flamel.ai](https://flamel.ai) and [Jurgy](https://jurgy.co).
- **Learning**: Notes on books, talks, and ideas that have shaped my thinking.

Stay tuned.
